About SpeedGuard.AI

SpeedGuard.AI represents the convergence of cutting-edge artificial intelligence and traffic safety technology. Our system employs YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection algorithm for real-time vehicle identification and EasyOCR for precise license plate recognition, enabling accurate point-to-point speed calculation.
